# Image-Gen Prompts

Concept image only; not fabrication authority. Do not include cut-ready dimensions, hole locations, or toolpaths.

## Hero Prompt

Photorealistic 3/4 product photograph of a finished small clavichord on a clean neutral light-grey studio background: a low, shallow rectangular wooden case with the long horizontal silhouette of a tabletop keyboard instrument, lid raised to reveal the interior. The recognizable signature is the tangent key action — a row of slim balanced wooden key levers running front-to-back, dark sharps among pale natural key fronts, each lever rising at the rear to lift a slender upright brass tangent into a string from below, so the line of polished tangents reads as the boundary where keys meet the stringing rather than a piano's struck-hammer action. Inside the case, strings run roughly parallel above a thin spruce-toned soundboard toward a bridge, a hitch field, and a row of tuning pins; soft cloth damping strips sit on the non-speaking string segments near the hitch end. Materials implied by the design: warm oiled close-grained hardwood case and keyframe, brass tangents and hardware catching a bright line of light across the action, precise but handmade joinery. Realistic furniture-scale cues so it reads as a bench- or table-top instrument, soft directional studio light raking gently across the keys to model the brass tangents, about a 50mm lens, high detail, photorealistic, no text labels.

## In-Context Prompt

Photorealistic image of the concept clavichord staged on a small luthier's workshop table beside calipers, a one-key tangent test coupon, a few spare brass tangent blanks, and a tuning wrench, lid open, with a player gently pressing a single key so the brass tangent stays in contact with its string. Warm natural window light rakes across the keyboard so the tangents glint along the rear of the keys; the quiet shop setting, visible strings and cloth damping, and the held-key gesture emphasize touch, after-pressure, and serviceable adjustment. Shallow depth of field keeps the key action and the line of tangents sharp while the workshop dissolves into warm bokeh; concept visualization only, no finalized scale or string count implied.

## Detail Prompt

Macro photorealistic close-up of the signature tangent contact: a slender brass tangent just touching a string from below where it both excites the string and forms the speaking-length boundary, the wooden key lever partly depressed, non-speaking string segments muted by a narrow cloth damping strip behind the contact point. Show the polished brass blade catching a bright specular highlight, the grain of the hardwood key lever, fine taut string and windings crossing the brass edge, and the tiny gap around adjacent strings. Soft raking side light revealing the contact point, shallow focus on the brass-meets-string boundary, high detail with textures of brass, wood, cloth, and wire — no measurements, no coordinate markings.

## Negative / Avoid

No text, watermarks, brand marks, or fake maker marks; no dimension lines or callouts; no piano hammers or felt-headed hammers (this is a tangent action, not a struck-hammer action); no impossible action geometry or colliding extra keys/strings; anatomically plausible hands if a player is shown.
